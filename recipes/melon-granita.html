Melon Granita

Overview:

A fresh, no-cook dessert for summer.

Ingredients:

  • 1 small melon,
  • Watermelon rougly the same amount,
  • A leaf of basil.
  • Half a lemon.

Preparation:

  1. Remove the seeds from the watermelon.
  2. Blend until no chunks remain.
  3. Strain through a wire mesh.
  4. Remove the seeds from the melon.
  5. Blend the melon, but don't strain.
  6. Mix them in a freezer-safe container.
  7. Chop the basil and mix it in.
  8. Freeze.

When the granita is set, remove with a fork. You can use the skin of the melon to serve the granita. Make sure you squeeze some lemon on top, and optionally add some lemon zest too!
