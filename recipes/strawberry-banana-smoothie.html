Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Overview:

A fresh and sweet smoothie for a quick morning fix.

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana,
  • 2 strawberries,
  • Oats,
  • Optional: Walnuts or almonds,
  • A cup of milk.

Preparation:

  1. Place the oats (and optional nuts) in the blender and blend until powdery.
  2. Add in the rest of the ingredients and blend until smooth.
  3. Taste for consistency and taste, then adjust the ingredients from the second step if necessary.
  4. Serve in a glass with some granola on top.
