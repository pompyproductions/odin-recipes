Tuna Pasta Revisited [DRAFT]

Overview:

An elevated version of an originally simple student-dish: Pasta con tonno e cipolla.

Ingredients:

  • [SAUCE] 2 cans of tuna,
  • [SAUCE] Mustard seeds,
  • [SAUCE] Ginger,
  • [SAUCE] 1 Onion,
  • [SAUCE+CREAM] Olive oil,
  • [CREAM] 1 slice of anchovy,
  • [CREAM] Turnip greens,
  • [CREAM] Corn (canned is fine),
  • [CREAM] Coriander seeds,
  • Pasta (you can use different kinds).

Preparation:

  1. [SAUCE] Caramelize onions with ginger in a pan.
  2. [CREAM] Heat up olive oil and coriander seeds in a saucepan.
  3. [CREAM] When it's sizzling, add the anchovy.
  4. Boil water in a pot, salt it, put the pasta in.
  5. [CREAM] Add turnip greens and corn in the saucepan.
  6. [SAUCE] Add the tuna to the caramelized onions.
  7. Take some of the pasta cooking water and add to both SAUCE and CREAM.
  8. [CREAM] Blend until smooth.
  9. Drain the pasta (put some water aside).
  10. If necessary, adjust the CREAM and SAUCE with some of that water.
  11. Mix the pasta in with the SAUCE.

It's done! Plate it as follows:

  1. Flat plate. Add some CREAM and spread it evenly by tapping the bottom of the plate.
  2. Put pasta on top.
  3. Top it off with some black pepper and toasted breadcrumbs.
